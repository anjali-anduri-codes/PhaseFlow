# FlowFit - Quick Start Guide

Welcome to FlowFit! Here's how to explore all the features:

## 🎯 First Time Setup

1. **Welcome Screen** - Read the tagline and click "Get started"
2. **Data Source** - Choose how to sync your cycle (try "Flo app")
3. **Cycle Dates** - Enter your last cycle start date and typical cycle length
4. **Goals** - Select your fitness goals (you can pick multiple)
5. You're in! The app opens to the **Home** screen

---

## 🏠 Home Screen - Your Command Center

The home screen shows:
- **Weekly Stats** - Your streak, workouts this week, and level
- **Phase Card** - Current cycle day and phase with energy indicators
- **Energy Check-In** - Rate how you feel today (Drained → Strong)
- **Recommended Workout** - AI-suggested workout for your phase
- **Quick Actions** - Fast access to Chat and Log

### Try This:
- Tap the **"Start workout"** button to begin a live workout
- Click **"Ask Gemma"** to chat with your AI coach
- Use **"Browse all"** to see the full workout library

---

## 💪 Workout Features

### Browse Workouts
1. Tap **"Workouts"** in the bottom navigation
2. Filter by category: Strength, Cardio, Flexibility, Recovery
3. See workout duration, intensity, and recommended phase
4. Tap any workout to start

### During a Workout
- **Large timer** shows time remaining for current exercise
- **Progress bar** at top shows overall workout completion
- **Play/Pause** button to control timing
- **Skip button** to move to next exercise
- **Chat icon** (top-right) to ask Gemma for form tips mid-workout!

### After a Workout
- View **completion stats** (duration, exercises, phase)
- Rate how you're feeling
- Add notes to your workout log (optional)

---

## 💬 Chat with Gemma AI

**3 Ways to Access:**
1. Tap **"Chat"** in bottom navigation
2. Click **"Ask Gemma"** quick action on Home
3. Tap chat icon **during a workout** for real-time advice

### What You Can Ask:
- "What exercises are best for my phase?"
- "I feel tired today, what should I do?"
- "How can I reduce bloating?"
- "Modify my workout for lower energy"
- "Is this the right form for planks?"

The chat shows **suggested prompts** to get you started!

**Privacy Note**: All AI processing happens on-device by default (see Settings)

---

## 📅 Calendar

- View your full month at a glance
- Each day is colored by phase:
  - **Red tint** = Menstrual
  - **Green tint** = Follicular
  - **Purple tint** = Ovulatory
  - **Amber tint** = Luteal
- **Small dots** below dates = logged workout
- **Ring around date** = today
- See upcoming phase preview at bottom

---

## 📝 Workout Logging

### Quick Log from Home:
1. Tap **"Log workout"** quick action
2. Type naturally: "Felt stronger than expected, finished all sets but was tired"
3. Tap **"Analyse with Gemma 4"**
4. AI extracts: energy level, completion %, mood
5. Review and save

### After Completing a Workout:
- Completion screen auto-fills workout data
- Just add how you're feeling and any notes

---

## ⚙️ Settings

Access via **gear icon** on Home or from bottom nav on other screens.

### Cycle Data Sources:
- Connected sources shown with green badge
- Disconnect or add new sources

### AI & Privacy:
- **On-device processing** (recommended) - data never leaves your phone
- **Cross-device sync** - optional Supabase integration

## 🎨 Component Library

Want to see all the UI components?

1. Click the **book icon** (📖) in the top-right corner (any screen)
2. Browse all component variants:
   - Phase Cards for each phase
   - Workout cards in different states
   - Energy check-in buttons
   - Badges, progress bars, inputs
   - Calendar day states

This is useful for developers or if you're curious about the design system!

## 🔑 Navigation Cheat Sheet

### Bottom Navigation:
- **Home** 🏠 - Dashboard and stats
- **Workouts** 💪 - Browse workout library
- **Calendar** 📅 - View cycle and logged workouts
- **Chat** 💬 - Ask Gemma AI anything

### Common Actions:
- **Start a workout**: Home → Recommended workout → Start workout
- **Browse workouts**: Workouts tab → Pick category → Select
- **Ask AI**: Chat icon (or quick action) → Type question → Get answer
- **Log workout**: Home → Log workout → Write notes → Analyse
- **View settings**: Gear icon on Home

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Rate your energy daily** - The AI learns your patterns and improves recommendations
2. **Use natural language in logs** - Write "felt great, finished everything" instead of structured data
3. **Ask Gemma during workouts** - Get real-time form corrections and modifications
4. **Check phase tips** - Small cards on Home with nutrition and recovery advice
5. **Browse by intensity** - In luteal/menstrual phase? Filter for "Recovery" workouts
